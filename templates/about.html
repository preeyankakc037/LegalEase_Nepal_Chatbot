About LegalEase Nepal

Our mission is to bridge the justice gap in Nepal's informal sector through accessible technology

Who We Are

LegalEase Nepal is an innovative legal tech initiative focused on making legal knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of income or education level.

Our team combines legal expertise with cutting-edge AI technology to create solutions that empower Nepal's small business owners and independent workers.

The Problem We Solve

In Nepal, over 60% of the workforce operates in the informal sector with little to no access to affordable legal services.

This leaves small vendors, freelancers, and micro-entrepreneurs vulnerable to exploitation, contract disputes, and legal challenges they don't understand.

Our Innovative Solution

How LegalEase Nepal is transforming access to justice through technology

AI-Powered Legal Assistant

Our chatbot platform provides instant, reliable legal advice in simple language, available 24/7 through multiple channels including WhatsApp and web chat.

Key features include:

  • Multilingual support (Nepali and English)
  • Document generation for contracts and legal forms
  • Legal compliance checking
  • Step-by-step guidance for common legal processes

Our Impact

Advancing the UN Sustainable Development Goals through innovation

Goal 8: Decent Work & Economic Growth

By providing legal protection to informal workers, we enable sustainable economic opportunities and help formalize Nepal's growing gig economy.

Our platform helps users:

  • Create legally sound contracts
  • Understand labor rights
  • Navigate business registration
  • Resolve payment disputes

Goal 16: Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions

We're reducing barriers to justice by making legal knowledge freely available and helping users navigate complex systems.

Key contributions:

  • Democratizing access to legal information
  • Reducing case backlogs through alternative resolution
  • Promoting legal literacy
  • Supporting judicial system digitization

Product Development

From concept to working prototype

Prototype Journey

Our initial prototype focused on core functionality to validate the concept with real users in Kathmandu's informal markets.

Key prototype features included:

  • Basic Q&A for common legal questions
  • Simple contract generator
  • User feedback system
  • Basic Nepali language support

After successful testing with 150+ users, we're now developing the full platform with expanded capabilities.
